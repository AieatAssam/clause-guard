❓ What this is — and isn't

This is: A pattern-matching tool for spotting common contractual red flags. Think of it like a spell-checker for contracts. It flags known patterns and explains why they matter.

This isn't: Legal advice. ClauseGuard does not replace a qualified attorney. It helps you know what to ask your lawyer about — and gives you the vocabulary to have that conversation.

⚖️ No attorney-client relationship. Use of this tool does not create an attorney-client relationship. The analysis is based on automated pattern matching only and is not comprehensive. It may miss critical issues, flag harmless clauses as risky, or fail to account for jurisdiction-specific laws. This tool's output is not legally enforceable and should not be relied upon as legal advice. Always consult a qualified legal professional for contract review tailored to your specific circumstances and jurisdiction.
